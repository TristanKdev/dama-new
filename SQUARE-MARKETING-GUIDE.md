# DAM:A — Square Marketing Guide

## How Leads Get Into Square

Every person who interacts with your website is automatically saved as a **Square Customer**. This happens when someone:

- **Places an order** (name, email, phone saved)
- **Submits a contact form** (name, email saved)
- **Requests catering** (name, email, phone + event details saved)
- **Signs up for the newsletter** (email saved)
- **Starts a subscription** (name, email, card on file saved)

No duplicates are created — if someone orders twice, they show up once in Square with all their info.

## Sending Marketing Emails & Texts via Square

### Step 1: Open Square Dashboard

Go to **https://squareup.com/dashboard** and log in.

### Step 2: View Your Customers

1. Click **Customers** in the left sidebar
2. You'll see everyone who has interacted with your site
3. Each customer shows their name, email, phone, and notes (what they did — "Order", "Catering inquiry", "Newsletter signup", etc.)

### Step 3: Create Customer Groups

1. Go to **Customers > Directory**
2. Click **Create Group**
3. Use filters to segment:
   - **All customers** — everyone
   - **Ordered before** — repeat customers
   - **Has email** — for email campaigns
   - **Has phone** — for text campaigns
   - **Notes contain "Catering"** — catering leads
   - **Notes contain "Newsletter"** — newsletter subscribers
   - **Created in last 30 days** — recent leads

### Step 4: Send Email Campaigns

1. Go to **Marketing** in the left sidebar (or **https://squareup.com/dashboard/marketing**)
2. Click **Create Campaign > Email**
3. Choose your audience (a customer group or all customers)
4. Design your email using Square's editor
5. Schedule or send immediately

**Campaign ideas:**
- Weekly menu announcement (every Monday)
- New item launches
- Seasonal specials
- Holiday promotions
- Catering follow-ups

### Step 5: Send Text (SMS) Campaigns

1. Go to **Marketing > Create Campaign > Text Message**
2. Select your audience
3. Write your message (160 characters max for best delivery)
4. Send or schedule

**Text message ideas:**
- "This week's banchan just dropped — order by 10 PM tonight for Tuesday delivery"
- "New seasonal tteok available this week only"
- "Catering for your next event? Reply YES for a custom quote"

## Automated Square Emails

Square can also send **automatic** emails. Set these up in **Marketing > Automations**:

- **Welcome email** — sent when a new customer is created
- **Win-back email** — sent to customers who haven't ordered in X days
- **Birthday email** — if you collect birthdays
- **Review request** — after a purchase

## Where Customer Notes Come From

Each customer in Square has a **note** that tells you how they found you:

| Note | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| `Order: A1B2C3D4` | Placed an order (order ID included) |
| `Contact: General Inquiry` | Submitted the contact form |
| `Catering: Corporate Event, 50 guests, 2026-04-15` | Requested catering |
| `Newsletter signup` | Signed up for the newsletter |

---

## Tips

- **Export customers**: In Square Dashboard, go to Customers > Export to get a CSV of all leads
- **Square Marketing is free** for up to 500 emails/month on the Free plan
- **Text marketing** requires Square Marketing Plus ($15/mo) for automated texts
- **All leads are deduped** by email — a person who orders AND signs up for the newsletter appears once
- **Phone numbers** are captured from orders and catering forms, making those leads SMS-ready
